Monkey king

The Monkey King is a 2023 computer-animated fantasy action comedy film directed by Anthony Stacchi. It is a prequel to the classic Ming dynasty novel Journey to the West. The film stars Jimmy O. Yang, voicing over the role of the titular trickster, with Bowen Yang, Jo Koy, BD Wong, Jolie Hoang-Rappaport, and Stephanie Hsu in supporting roles.[1] The Monkey King was selected as the closing film at the 22nd New York Asian Film Festival, where it had its world premiere on July 30, 2023[2], and was released worldwide on August 18, 2023, by Netflix. The film received mixed reviews from critics.
